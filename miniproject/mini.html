About Paws & Hearts 🐾

We believe every street dog deserves a loving home and a second chance at happiness

Our Mission 🎯

We started this initiative because we witnessed the harsh reality of street dogs - hungry, scared, and longing for love. Our mission is to bridge the gap between these beautiful souls and families looking for loyal companions.

Every dog deserves a warm bed, regular meals, medical care, and most importantly, unconditional love. We work tirelessly to make this a reality.

Our Vision 🌟

We envision a world where no dog has to sleep on cold streets or search for food in garbage bins. A world where every wagging tail has a home to return to.

Through adoption, education, and community support, we're working towards creating a society that values and protects all animals.

Free Food Distribution Program 🍖

We organize regular food distribution camps to ensure no street dog goes hungry. Join us in feeding our four-legged friends!

Our Food Program 📅

Central Park Area

Every Sunday, 8:00 AM - 10:00 AM

Regular feeding spot for 15-20 dogs in the Central Park vicinity.

Market Street

Every Wednesday, 6:00 PM - 7:30 PM

Evening feeding session for dogs around the busy market area.

Riverside Colony

Every Saturday, 7:00 AM - 9:00 AM

Morning feeding for dogs in the residential colony area.
